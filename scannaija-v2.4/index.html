About ScanNaija
ScanNaija v2.1 — Barcode & Product Security Platform
Built to help Nigerian manufacturers protect their products from counterfeiting.

Product data is synced to a shared server registry. Buyers can verify any product via the Buyer Verify portal. Piracy flags from buyers automatically alert the manufacturer.
